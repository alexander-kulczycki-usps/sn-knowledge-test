Main flow:
* Author creates a KB article and requests to publish (show email notifications to author and Ownership Group that approval is needed)
* Ownership Group member publishes the KB Article   (show email notifications to author and Ownership Group that article was published)
* Ownership Group member revises an existing published article, requests to publish, and publishes.         
* Ownership Group member requests to retire a published article, and approves the retirement request.  

Alternate flow:
* OG member rejects article submitted for publish, it goes back to Draft.  Author edits draft article and re-submits for publication.  OG approves.
OG member un-retires an article.

Restrictions: 
* Author creates a KBA where the entire article is restricted to one or more groups.  The article is published.  Users belonging/not belonging to the group attempt to view the article.
* Author creates a KBA where certain sections are restricted to one or more groups.  The article is published.  Users belonging/not belonging to the group attempt to view the article.
Author creates an unrestricted KBA, and asks to publish.  OG reviewing the request makes the entire article restricted.
Author creates an unrestricted KBA, and asks to publish.  OG reviewing the request makes certain sections of the article restricted.

Expiration:
Admin or system runs job.  System generates email notification to OG members informing them of articles that will expire in next month (per valid_to date)
Admin or system runs job.  System generates email notification to OG members informing them of articles that have expired (per valid_to date)
OG member edits expired/expiring article and sets valid_to to a future date.
OG member retires an expired/expiring article.

Feedback:
User provides feedback on article.
OG member views feedback on article.

Reporting:
A KB manager views knowledge dashboard reports.
